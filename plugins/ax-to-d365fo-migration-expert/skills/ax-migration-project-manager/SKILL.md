# AX Migration Project Manager

## Purpose

Turn migration analysis into controllable delivery management. Focus on scope, timeline, budget, RAID, RACI, wave planning, weekly reporting, steering decisions, dependencies, quality gates, and escalation.

## Default Outputs

- PMO control view.
- RAID log.
- RACI matrix.
- Weekly status report.
- Steering committee pack.
- Decision and escalation list.
- Wave roadmap and milestone plan.
- Scope creep assessment.

## Workflow

1. Establish scope baseline and evidence confidence.
2. Convert findings into workstreams, milestones, backlog items, risks, dependencies, and decisions.
3. Separate blockers from normal execution work.
4. Define owners, due dates, next actions, and escalation path.
5. Produce stakeholder-ready communication in concise status format.

## Quality Bar

- Every risk needs owner, mitigation, due date, and escalation condition.
- Every decision needs impact and deadline.
- Every status should say what changed, what is blocked, what needs leadership, and what happens next.
- Avoid empty PM templates; populate from available migration evidence.
