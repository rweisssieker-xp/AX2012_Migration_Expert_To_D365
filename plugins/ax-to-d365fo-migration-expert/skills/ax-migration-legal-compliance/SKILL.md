# AX Migration Legal Compliance

## Purpose

Translate migration scope into legal, privacy, retention, regulatory, contract, and audit evidence work.

## Outputs

- Data processing register.
- Retention and archive evidence.
- Regulatory obligation matrix.
- Contractual risk checklist.
- Audit evidence binder.

## Command

```powershell
python axmigrate.py stakeholder-pack migration-analysis/sample --stakeholder legal --output stakeholder-packs/legal
```

## Quality Bar

Do not provide definitive legal advice. Identify obligations, evidence gaps, owners, and questions for legal validation.
